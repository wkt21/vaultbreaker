Welcome to FileVault Secure Documents
======================================

FileVault is the internal document platform for staff. Pages are rendered
through the document viewer at view.php?file=<name>.

If you have been issued staff credentials, you may sign in at /login.php to
access the administrative console, including the secure file upload facility.

NOTE TO ADMINS: backups run nightly. The application database and configuration
are stored locally on this host. Do not leave credentials in shared documents.
